ðŸ“… Detailed 7 Days Tour Itinerary

A perfectly planned day-by-day schedule to ensure you don't miss anything.

Your journey begins with excitement! Assemble at Gorakhpur Railway Station where you will meet the representative from Complete Complete travel Solutions. Board the train (Mour Dhwaj Express / Amarnath Express as per schedule) for an overnight journey to Jammu Tawi. Relax and enjoy the journey with your group/family as you move from the plains of UP towards the foothills of the Himalayas.

Upon arrival at Jammu Tawi Railway Station, our driver will greet you. We begin our road trip to Srinagar (approx 260km / 8-9 hours). This drive is an experience in itself! You will pass through the famous Banihal Tunnel and witness the sudden change in landscape from rocky mountains to the lush green Kashmir valley. We will stop at the Titanic View Point for your first breathtaking photo of the valley. Arrive in Srinagar by evening, check into your Deluxe Hotel. Dinner and overnight stay.

Start your day with a delicious breakfast. Today we explore the beauty of Srinagar.
1. Mughal Gardens: Visit Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure), Shalimar Bagh (Abode of Love), and Chashme Shahi (Royal Spring). These gardens are famous for their terraced lawns and cascading fountains.
2. Shankaracharya Temple: Located on a hill, seek blessings and enjoy a panoramic bird's eye view of the entire Srinagar city.
3. Shikara Ride: In the evening, check into a traditional wooden Houseboat on Dal Lake. Enjoy a complimentary 1-Hour Shikara Ride, witnessing the floating market and the golden sunset over the lake.
Dinner and a unique overnight stay in the Houseboat.

After breakfast, proceed for a day trip to Gulmarg (approx 50km). Gulmarg is famous for its ski slopes and the highest golf course in the world.
The Journey: The drive passes through Tangmarg and offers views of pine forests.
Gondola Ride: You can take the famous Gondola Cable Car ride (tickets at own cost). Phase 1 takes you to Kongdori, and Phase 2 takes you to Apharwat Peak where you can touch the snow throughout the year.
Return to Srinagar in the evening. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Today we drive to Pahalgam. On the way, visit the Saffron fields of Pampore and the historic Avantipura Ruins. You can also visit a cricket bat factory to see how famous Willow bats are made.
In Pahalgam: Enjoy the mesmerizing flow of the Lidder River. You can hire a local union cab (at own cost) to visit Betaab Valley (famous for Bollywood shoots), Chandanwari (starting point of Amarnath Yatra), and Aru Valley.
Alternatively, you can take a pony ride to Baisaran Valley (Mini Switzerland). Return to Srinagar in the evening for dinner and rest.

After a hearty breakfast, check out from the hotel. We begin our return journey towards Jammu. On the way, you can stop at local shops to buy world-famous Kashmiri Dry Fruits, Saffron, and Pashmina Shawls as souvenirs for your loved ones. Arrive at Jammu Tawi Railway Station in the evening. Board the train for your return journey to Gorakhpur.

Arrive at Gorakhpur Railway Station in the afternoon/evening with bags full of shopping and hearts full of beautiful memories. We hope you enjoyed the hospitality of Complete Complete travel Solutions. We look forward to serving you again!
