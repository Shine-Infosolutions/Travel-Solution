Gorakhpur to Prayagraj (Allahabad) - 3 Days Tour Package

Experience the divine spirituality of Prayagraj (formerly Allahabad) with Complete Travel Solution's exclusively curated 3-day tour package starting from Gorakhpur. Known as the 'King of Pilgrimages' (Tirtharaj), Prayagraj is situated at the holy confluence of three rivers: the Ganga, the Yamuna, and the invisible Saraswati.

We offer a completely hassle-free journey where we pick you up directly from Gorakhpur Railway Station, Airport, or your residence in a private, sanitized AC Cab. Whether you are visiting for the Pind Daan rituals, the holy bath at Sangam, or to explore the historical heritage of the Nehru family, this tour covers it all with comfort and safety.

Key Attractions You Will Visit

  • Triveni Sangam: The sacred point where the muddy waters of the Ganges meet the clear green water of the Yamuna. It is believed that a holy dip here washes away all sins.
  • Bade Hanuman Mandir: A unique temple situated near the Sangam featuring a 20-foot long idol of Lord Hanuman in a reclining (sleeping) position.
  • Alopi Devi Shakti Peeth: A revered temple where no idol is worshipped, but a wooden carriage (doli) is worshipped as the manifestation of Goddess Sati.
  • Anand Bhawan: The historic ancestral home of the Nehru-Gandhi family, now converted into a museum showcasing the history of India's freedom struggle.
  • Chandrashekhar Azad Park: A massive park where the freedom fighter Chandrashekhar Azad attained martyrdom.

Detailed Itinerary (Day Wise Plan)

Morning: Our driver will pick you up from your desired location in Gorakhpur (Railway Station / Airport / Home). We will begin our journey to Prayagraj via NH-24 or NH-19 (Approx. 230 KM / 5-6 Hours).

Afternoon: Arrival in Prayagraj. Check-in to your pre-booked hotel and rest for a while to recover from the journey.

Evening: We will take you to the riverside. Enjoy the serene view of the Ganges during sunset. You can stroll around the Ghats and absorb the spiritual atmosphere.
Night: Dinner (on your own) and overnight stay at the hotel in Prayagraj.

Early Morning: Proceed to Triveni Sangam (the confluence of Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati) for a holy dip and rituals. Afterward, visit:

  • Bade Hanuman Ji (Reclining Hanuman) Temple.
  • Alopi Devi Shakti Peeth Temple.
  • View of Allahabad Fort and Akshayavat (from outside).
In the evening, you will have free time to explore the local markets or Civil Lines. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Morning: Enjoy a fresh breakfast at the hotel and check-out.

Sightseeing: Visit Anand Bhawan (Museum of the Nehru Family) and the adjacent Swaraj Bhawan. If time permits, you can also visit Khusro Bagh (Mughal-era tombs).

Return Journey: Post sightseeing, we start our drive back to Gorakhpur. We will drop you off at Gorakhpur Railway Station or your home by evening. Complete Travel Solution thanks you for choosing us for your spiritual journey.
